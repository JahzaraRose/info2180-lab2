My Journey in Computer Science

October 5, 2024 by Jahzara Rose

As a computer science major, I often get asked what drew me to this field. The truth is, the money, from a young age I've watched my uncle thrive in this field allowing him to now live that stable lifestyle we all dream for. Despite that I found technology fascinating with how it shapes our world. From creating my first website to diving into algorithms and data structures,with each exhilarating step I actually found my passion.

One of the things I love most about coding is the problem-solving aspect. Every line of code is a challenge waiting to be tackled. Whether I’m debugging an error or optimizing a program, I find immense satisfaction in finding solutions. It’s like solving a puzzle, and each piece fits together to create something functional and meaningful. Don't get me wrong it is very frustrating when a piece that should fit doesn't. What I hate the most is the fact that there is always a due date haunting me.

Hence, In future posts, I’ll be sharing tips for fellow students, project highlights, and my thoughts on emerging technologies like AI.
